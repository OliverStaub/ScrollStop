Privacy First

All blocking happens locally on your device. No data is sent to external servers.

How It Works

  1. Monitor: ScrollStop monitors your scrolling behavior on blocked sites
  2. Detect: When excessive scrolling is detected, a warning animation appears
  3. Block: The site is blocked for 60 minutes with a countdown timer
  4. Redirect: You're encouraged to do something more productive instead

Supported Sites

ScrollStop currently blocks doomscrolling on these popular social media platforms:

  • Facebook
  • Twitter/X
  • Instagram
  • Reddit
  • TikTok
  • YouTube
  • LinkedIn
  • Pinterest
