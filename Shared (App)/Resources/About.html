About ScrollStop

Version: 1.0

Developer: Oliver Staub

Description

ScrollStop is a Safari extension that helps you break free from endless scrolling and doomscrolling habits on social media websites. When you scroll too much, it blocks the site for 60 minutes and encourages you to do something more productive.

Credits & Attribution
